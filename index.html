About Us

We are a group of 9 curious, driven students between the ages of 13-17 with a diverse set of experiences, but a shared interest in making an impact through the fascinating world of technology and robotics.

  • We have weekly in person meetings where we work collectively, as well as a weekly virtual meeting to organize goals and report progress.
  • We communicate through discord to optimize efficiency so we can consistently be aware of the state of our robot and collaborate better.
  • Our team is segmented into programming, mechanics, design, and outreach however our roles are often flexible and we all contribute to each aspect.

What is FIRST?

Visit FIRST's website


FIRST is a non-profit organization that organizes robotic competitions such as First Robotics Competition, First Tech Challenge, FIRST LEGO League Challenge, FIRST LEGO League Explore, and FIRST LEGO League Discover. Below is a summary of FIRST's mission:
  • To inspire young people to be science and technology leaders and innovators
  • To help them build skills relating to STEM
  • To inspire innovation
  • To build on life skills such as self-confidence, communication, and leadership

The Pr0Teens is a team competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC).
